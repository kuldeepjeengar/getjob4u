About getjob4u

getjob4u is a free career toolkit for AI, ML, and Data Science aspirants — built by one engineer who got tired of seeing job seekers waste money on paid tools that don't actually help.

KJ

Hi, I'm Kuldeep 👋

I'm an engineer who's lived the AI / ML / Data Science job-hunt firsthand — the silent ATS rejections, the LinkedIn cold outreach grind, the resume rewrites at 2 AM the night before a deadline. I built getjob4u to give job seekers the tools I wish I had: an honest ATS scanner, real interview prep, working email templates, and curated learning resources — completely free, no signup, no ads.

If this site saves you even one hour, it was worth building.
